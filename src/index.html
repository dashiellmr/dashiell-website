Dashiell is an undergraduate studying computer science and linguistics at McGill University in Montréal, Canada. In the past, he was a software engineer, an editor, a computer vision researcher, and a journalist. His research interests primarily lie in the semantics of natural language, logic, and computational linguistics broadly. Outside of school, he is an avid participant in hackathons, creates tools that others may find useful, and enjoys drawing syntax trees.
